Gautama Siddhartha

Gautama Siddhartha, known as Buddha, is a powerful and cool-headed character in Record of Ragnarok. Originally human, he was a prince who lived in luxury before discovering the true meaning of happiness.

After leaving behind his throne, family, and wealth, he wandered the world and gained enlightenment. Later, he ascended to godhood and became one of the few divine beings who supports humanity.

Ring of the Twelve Realms
